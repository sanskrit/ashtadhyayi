---
sutra: मतोः पूर्वमात्संज्ञायां स्त्रियाम्
vRtti: मतोः पूर्व आकार उदात्तो भवति तच्चेन्मत्वन्तं स्त्रीलिङ्ग संज्ञा भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The आ before the affix मत् has the acute accent, when the word is a name in the Feminine Gender.

---
Thus अदुम्बरा꣡वती, पुष्करा꣡वती, शरा꣡वती (4.2.85). The lengthening takes place by (6.3.120). वीरणा꣡वती ॥ Why do we say 'the आ'? Observe इक्षुम꣡ती, द्रुमवती꣡ ॥ The words इक्षु _Un_. III. 157 and द्रुम (5.2.108) are end-acute, so accent is on मतुप् by (VI. I. I76). Why do we say when a name? Observe ख꣡ट्वावती ॥ खट्वा is formed by क्वन् and has acute on the first (_Un_. I. 151). Why do we say in the Feminine Gender? Observe शरावान् ॥ Why do we say when followed by मत् ? Observe गवादिनी ॥
