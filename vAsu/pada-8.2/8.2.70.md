---
sutra: अम्नरूधरवरित्युभयथा छन्दसि
vRtti: अम्नस् ऊधस् अवस् इत्येतेषां छन्दसि विषये उभयथा भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: "In the _Chhandas_, both रु and र are substituted for the final of _amnas_, _udhas_, and _avas_."
---
Thus अम्न एव or अम्नरेव, ऊध एव or ऊधरेव; अव एव or अवरेव ॥ When रु is substituted for the finals, this रु is replaced by य् by (VIII. 3. 17), which is elided by (VIII. 3. 19). The word अम्नस् means 'a little', and अवस् 'protection'.

Vart:- रु and र both replace the final of प्रचेतस् before राजन्, in the Vedic as well as in the secular language. As प्रचेता राजन् (VIII. 3. 14), (VI. 3. 111) प्रचेतो राजन् ॥

Vart:- The words अहर् &c before पति &c should be enumerated. That is, the finals of अहर् &c are replaced by रु or र before पति &c. As. अहर्पतिः or अहᳶपतिः or अहः पतिः, अहर्पुत्रः, अहᳶपुत्रः, अहः पुत्रः, गीर्पतिः, गीः पति, गीᳶपतिः; धूर्पति, धूः पतिः, धूᳵपतिः ॥ Here र् is substituted for the final र् of अहर् &c, which at first sight may appear superfluous. But it is so ordained, in order to prevent the _visarga_ change of this र ॥
