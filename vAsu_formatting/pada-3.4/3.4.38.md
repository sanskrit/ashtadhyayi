---
sutra: स्नेहने पिषः
vRtti: स्नेहवाचिनि करण उपपदे पिषेर्द्धातोर्णमुल् प्रत्ययो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The affix '_namul_' comes after the verb '_pish_' (to grind), when a word in the Instrumental case denoting 'liquid' is in composition.

---
Thus उदपेषं पिनष्टि = उदकेन पिनष्ठि (6.3.58.) 'he grinds with water'; तैलपेषं पिनष्टि 'he grinds with oil'. Here also (3.4.46) applies, and an appropriate verb from the same root is employed to govern the gerund.
