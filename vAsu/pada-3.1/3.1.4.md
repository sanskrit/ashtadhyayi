---
sutra: अनुदात्तौ सुप्पितौ
vRtti: सुपः पितश्च प्रत्यया अनुदात्ता भवन्ति ॥  
vRtti_eng: The case-affixes _(sup)_ and the affixes having an indicatory प _(pit)_ are _anudatta_.
---
This is an exception _(apavada)_ to the last aphorism. As दृसदौ 'two stones'; the case-affix औ is _anudatta_; so also तृपदः; so also पचति; The third person singular affix तिप् has an indicatory प, and is _anudatta_. But not पचतः.
