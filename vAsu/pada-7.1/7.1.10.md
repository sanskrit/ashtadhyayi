---
sutra: बहुलं छन्दसि
vRtti: छन्दसि विषये बहुलमैसादेशो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: In the _Veda_ the substitution takes place diversely.

---
That is ऐस् is substituted for भिस् even after stems which do not end in short अ; as नद्यः; and some times the substitution does not take place even after stems ending in short अ, as, देवेभिः सर्वेभिः प्रोक्तम् ॥ देवो देवोभिरागमत् (_Rig_ _Veda_ 1.1.4).
