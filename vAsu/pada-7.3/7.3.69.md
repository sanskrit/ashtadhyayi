---
sutra: भोज्यं भक्ष्ये
vRtti: भोज्यं निपात्यते भक्ष्येभिधेये ॥ भुजेर्ण्यति कुत्वाभावो निपात्यते शक्यार्थे ॥
vRtti_eng: "भोज्य preserves its palatal in the sense of 'eatable'."
---
The word is derived from भुज् with ण्यत् in the sense of 'able'. As भोज्य ओदनः 'eatable rice', भोज्या यवागूः 'eatable barley-gruel'. भक्ष्य here means anything which is fit for being eaten. When not having this sense, we have भोग्यः कम्बलः ॥
