---
sutra: कुम्भपदीषु च
vRtti: कुम्भपदीप्रभृतयः कृतपादलोपाः पठ्यन्ते समुदाया एव । तत्रैवं सूत्रं ज्ञेयम् । पादस्य लोपो भवति कुम्भपद्यादिविषये । यथा कुम्भपद्यादयः सिध्यन्ति ॥
vRtti_eng: The words _kumbhapadi_ &c, have been formed by the similar elision of the अ of पाद ॥

---
The forms कुंभपदी &c, are formed by eliding the final अ of पाद, and then substituting पद् for पात् before the feminine affix ई by (4.4.130). This substitution takes place only before the feminine affix ङीप्, and no where else, as कुम्भपदी, शतपदी ॥ Those words in the list, the first members of which denote an object of comparison or a numeral, would have elided अ by (5.4.138) or (5.4.140). In their case, this _sutra_ teaches the invariable affixing of ङीप् to such words; so that the optional rule (4.1.8) does not apply to them.
