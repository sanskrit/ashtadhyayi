---
sutra: स्वापेश्चङि
vRtti: स्वापेरिति स्वपेर्ण्यन्तस्य ग्रहणं तस्य चङि परतः संप्रसारणं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: Of the causative verb स्वापि 'to cause one to sleep', there is vocalisation of the semivowel, when the affix चङ् of the Reduplicated Aorist follows.

---
Thus the Aorist of स्वपि is असूषुपत्, असूषुपताम्, असूषुपन् ॥ The vocalisation takes place before reduplication, then there is _guna_ of the penultimate short vowel, then this is again shortened by (7.4.1), then there is reduplication, and then lengthening of the vowel of the reduplicate by (7.4.94). Thus स्वापि + चङ् = सुपि + चङ् = सुप् (6.4.51) + चङ् (7.3.86) = सोप् + चङ् = सुप् + चङ् (7.4.1) = सु + सुप् + चङ् (6.1.11) = सूषुपत् (7.4.94) which with the augment अ becomes असूषुपत् ॥  

Why do we say 'when चङ् follows'? Observe स्वाप्यते, स्वापितः ॥ The _anuvritti_ of किति has ceased, that of ङिति however is here.
