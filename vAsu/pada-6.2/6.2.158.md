---
sutra: आक्रोशे च
vRtti: आक्रोशे च गम्यमाने नञ उत्तरमच्क्रान्तमन्तोदात्तं भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: A word formed by the _krit_-affixes अच् or क, preceded by the Negative particle, has acute on the final when one abuses somebody by that word.
---  
Thus अपचोऽयं जाल्मः 'this rogue does not cook, though he can do so'. Here avarice is indicated, the fellow wants more pay before he will cook : and not his incapacity. So also अपठोऽयं जाल्मः, so also अविक्षिपः, अविलिखः ॥
