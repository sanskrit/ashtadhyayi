---
sutra: जयः करणम्
vRtti: जयशब्दः करणवाची आद्युदात्तो भवति ॥
vRtti_eng: The acute accent falls on the first syllable of जय, in the sense of 'whereby one attains victory'.

---
Thus ज꣡योऽश्वः, but otherwise जयो वर्त्तते ब्राह्मणानाम् ॥ The former जय is by प affix, (3.1.118) the second by अच् (3.2.31) ॥
